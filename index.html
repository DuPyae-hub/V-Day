A Letter for You

My love,

From the moment you came into my life, everything changed. You didn’t just walk in—you brought light, laughter, and a kind of peace I didn’t know I was missing. Every day with you feels like a gift.

I love your smile—the way it reaches your eyes and makes the whole room feel warmer. I love your kindness, how you care for the people around you without asking for anything back. I love our silly adventures, the inside jokes, the boba runs, and the quiet moments when we don’t need to say a word.

But more than any single thing, I love you. The person you are. The way you think, the way you laugh, the way you make me want to be a better person. You’re my favorite place to be.

So this Valentine’s Day, I just want you to know: you are loved, you are cherished, and you are the best part of my story. Here’s to many more December 30ths, and every ordinary, extraordinary day in between.

Forever yours,

With all my heart
